A Trust in Every Component

Rockman has been a trusted manufacturer of automotive components since 1960. Rockman manufactures 60 km of chains daily at its state-of-the-art chain manufacturing facility in Ludhiana. The company has in-house facility for tool development, heat treatment, fully automatic assembly line for the Automotive chains. Beyond chains Rockman is known for its high-quality brake shoes, shock absorbers, cone sets, and engine oil to other essential Automotive components. Manufactured using OEM-grade systems and high-quality materials to deliver Superior performance, durability, and long-term reliability on the road.

    Innovation & Process

    Engineering High Standards in Every Part

    We run our chain and sprocket production on automated lines using heat-treatment cycles. This setup increases the steel's fatigue strength and wear resistance, so the parts don't fail under high torque and stress. We apply the same manufacturing discipline here as we do for our best in class plants, maintaining the best industry specifications for material and performance for the Aftermarket.
